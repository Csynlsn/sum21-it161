AIA (Audience, Issues, and Approach)

Client Name Raven Bella Zingaro, friend
Client Bussiness Name Farrago Spiritum Tarot
New Website or Redesign Redesign of This Site
Website Type(s) Identity, Marketing, Shopping
Client Goals To create a secure, professional site while honoring the aesthetic and spirit of the deck
Audience Issues/Needs/Concerns Approach
Individuals: Browsing or Purchashing For Self

All genders expected, however high percentage will be female identifying. Most visitors will be using mobile devices or laptops. Ages ranging between 16 - 65, varying level of computer skills expected.
  • Need an attarctive and easy to navigate interface
  • Site must load quickly and as intended on different devices
  • Site must have secure check out
  • Site needs to appear modern and link to active social media accounts
  • Need to optimize mobile user experience
  • Site needs to be inclusive; we will have a diverse customer base
  • Different background images may load based on device capabilities to optimize browsing
  • Bandwitdth/device limitations will be considered when designing interface
  • Secure check out including Paypal option
  • Site needs to look sharp, modern, and relevant
  • Can incorporate user submitted images of customers and their purchase(s)
  • Can feature cards from the deck (weekly, monthly, daily) and sync this with social media accounts
Individual Buyers: Gift Buying

Ages ranging from 20 - 45, Men make up a higher percentage of this category. Can assume this demographic has basic computer competency and familiarity with online shopping
  • Need to grab users attention quickly; they may be casually browsing
  • Need excellent site navagation
  • Many will browse site using phones
  • May be buying as a gift for someone in another state or country
  • May be considering shipping time when deciding to buy
  • Make sure site remembers a returning user
  • Checkout area needs to be prominent and easy to use; no "sign up" required for simple check out
  • Optimize mobile experience
  • Offer gift receipts/packing slips
  • Offer fast shipping from reliable sources
Prospective Resellers:

Includes bookstores, oddities merchants, and online resellers. Ages range 30 - 65. Expected to have adequate computer skills.
  • Site needs to remember returning users
  • Will likely have the highest occurrence of tablet use.
  • May be considering purchasing in bulk, may expect larger price break
  • Buyers may be using forgien currency
  • Buyers may need assistance checking out/creating account
  • Site must load quickly and as intended on different devices
  • Will not force reseller accounts, but will only offer discounts to bulk buyers who create accounts
  • Will create option to "add" promotional items to ship out with deck. This would appear in the store near the product.
  • Offer seperate account for resellers
  • Have a troubleshooting and help area within the account sign up process
