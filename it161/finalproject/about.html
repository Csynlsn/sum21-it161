About the Farrago Spiritum Tarot

The Farrago Spiritum Tarot is the collaboration of Seattle based artist Yvette Endrijautzki and astrologer, tarot reader and teacher Raven Zingaro. The deck is an electronic assemblage of the artwork of Yvette is based on the writings of Raven. The two have been close friends for nearly 15 years and together feel a strong connection to this special project & getting it out into the world.
